Info about Stress



What is Stress?

  • Stress is a response to challenging feelings, situations, or responsibilities.

Research and Stats about Stress

  • Everyone experiences some degree of stress everyday

  • "About 33 percent of people report feeling extreme stress, 77 percent of people experience stress that affects their physical health, 73 percent of people have stress that impacts their mental health, and 48 percent of people have trouble sleeping because of stress"
    • Link to Info

How to Manage Stress and Why is Managing Stress Important?

  • You can manage stress by taking care of yourself and doing activities that you enjoy.

  • Managing stress is important because it can help you stay more productive while having some time off to yourself to relax and doing the things you enjoy.

Common Reasons of Stress

  • Family

  • Relationships

  • Peer Pressure, pressure from society

  • Work or academic balance with personal life
